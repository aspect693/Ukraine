Welcome

Welcome to the educational portal Ukraine&War — a resource dedicated to Ukraine's modern weaponry.

This site is created for anyone interested in military technology, defense capabilities, and the equipment that plays a key role in protecting our country. We have gathered the most up-to-date information about the latest types of weapons used by the Armed Forces of Ukraine, as well as materials covering current trends in the global military industry.


On the pages of Ukraine&War, you will find detailed reviews of various military equipment: from armored vehicles, artillery systems, and unmanned aerial vehicles to modern air defense systems. We offer not only analytical articles but also photos and videos that allow you to take a closer look at the weaponry in action.


We constantly update our news to keep you informed about the latest events concerning arms supplies, military reforms, and Ukraine's cooperation with international defense partners. Our experts provide analysis of the impact of new technologies on the course of military operations and the prospects for the development of Ukraine's defense industry.


Ukraine&War is not just a website; it’s a platform for discussing and understanding the importance of modern military equipment in defending our country. We strive to make military topics accessible and understandable to anyone interested in national security.


Join our community to stay updated on the latest achievements of Ukraine’s defense industry and get the newest information about the weaponry shaping the future of our army!
